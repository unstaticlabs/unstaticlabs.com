Unstatic Labs (USL) is a French-registered software engineering and consulting company with a varied range of activities performed as part of its incubation strategy. Notably, USL hosts marketing, content creation, holding, and products seller activities.

What We Do

Unstatic Labs primarily is a venture studio. We:

  1. Solve client problems — Clients come to us with business challenges. We scope and deliver solutions on a consulting basis.

  2. Build products — Solutions are developed as products that could serve a broader market, not just one client.

  3. Co-create companies — When there’s market opportunity, we spin off joint ventures where both parties co-invest and co-own.

Our interests are aligned with our clients because we’re building businesses together, not just delivering projects.
